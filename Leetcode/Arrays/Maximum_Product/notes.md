## 🔹 2. Maximum Product of Three Numbers

### 🧠 Problem
Find the maximum product of any 3 numbers in the array.

### 💡 Approach
- Sort the array
- Consider two cases:
  1. Product of 3 largest numbers
  2. Product of 2 smallest (negative) + largest number
- Return maximum of both

### ⏱ Complexity
- Time: O(n log n)
- Space: O(1)

### 🔑 Key Learning
- Handling negative numbers
- Edge cases matter more than logic complexity
- Sorting simplifies problems
